Registration page concept

Joining the practice should feel straightforward from the first screen.

Registration is often one of the most important conversion journeys on a GP website. This concept makes the process more welcoming, more explicit, and easier to understand before a patient starts.

1

Check eligibility and what you need

Show required documents, identity expectations, and any forms clearly before asking the patient to begin.

2

Submit registration information

Make it obvious whether the route is online, downloadable, or in person, and where the patient should send or bring details.

3

Understand what happens next

Explain likely review time, how confirmation will arrive, and when the patient can start using practice services or Patient Access.

Why this matters

Registration is a trust-building moment.

If a patient is moving practice, unhappy with previous care, or new to the area, this page should feel calm, competent, and welcoming. The more clearly the process is explained, the more likely the practice is to feel organised and approachable.
