Appointments

Appointments made simpler.

Patients should be able to tell in seconds whether they need a routine appointment, urgent same-day advice, online access, or out-of-hours help.

Urgent guidance: If your problem is urgent and cannot wait for the next routine appointment, call the practice on 01534 730541. If the practice is closed and urgent help is needed, call JDOC on 01534 445445.

Choose the route you need

The most useful appointment page is a decision surface, not a long policy note.

1

Routine appointment

For standard GP or nurse appointments, call reception or contact the practice during opening hours. Patients should be able to see this immediately from the top of the page.

Contact reception
2

Urgent same-day advice

If the problem cannot wait for the next routine appointment, patients should call the practice directly so the team can triage them appropriately.

Call 01534 730541
3

Online booking support

Where Patient Access is available, explain clearly what can be booked online and what still needs a phone call or receptionist help.

Use Patient Access

Telephone or in-person

Explain whether appointments may be telephone, face to face or triaged, and help patients understand what to expect.

Home visits

Clarify that home visits are usually for patients who are housebound or too unwell to travel, and encourage early calls where possible.

Changing or cancelling

Keep cancellation guidance short and visible so unused appointments can be released back to the practice promptly.

Out of hours

Place JDOC information high enough on the page to help anxious patients find the right next step when the surgery is closed.

Why this structure works

Action first, explanation second.

Patients rarely arrive on an appointments page wanting a full explanation of policy. They usually want to know what to do next, how urgently to act, and whether they can do it online.

  • Top of page: routine, urgent and out-of-hours routes.
  • Middle of page: booking format, visits and cancellations.
  • Lower on the page: operational detail and fuller notes if needed.
Recommended wording

Shorter copy under pressure

If your problem is urgent and cannot wait for the next routine appointment, please call us on 01534 730541 and let the reception team know that it is urgent.

That is quicker to scan than a dense paragraph while preserving the same operational meaning.
