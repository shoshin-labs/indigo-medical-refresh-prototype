Services

Care that is easier to browse.

Indigo Medical offers a wide range of general practice and specialist services. Grouping them by patient need makes the breadth of care feel easier to understand and navigate.

How patients think

Group services around needs, not internal labels.

Many visitors are searching for help with a family, screening or medication question rather than looking for an internal clinic category. The page should meet them in that mindset.

Tone

Polished, calm and still reassuring

Cleaner spacing, stronger hierarchy and more concise service cards help the breadth of care land without feeling cluttered.
