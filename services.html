Services overview refresh

Show the breadth of care without overwhelming the patient.

Indigo Medical’s public site already reflects a wide range of general practice and specialist services. This concept brings those together in a more scannable service overview, with stronger categorisation and cleaner navigation.

Service strategy

Group the offer around how patients think.

Patients often search by need rather than by internal clinic naming. A stronger services page can combine simple categories such as family care, preventive care, nurse clinics, and specialist support with more detailed pages beneath.

Tone

Modern, polished, still reassuring

The design direction here uses cleaner spacing, fewer visual interruptions, and more structured service cards so the breadth of care feels easier to take in.
