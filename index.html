What do you need today?
Routine appointment Book or request a standard GP or nurse appointment without digging through paragraphs first. Urgent same-day help Make the urgent pathway immediately clear, with guidance to call rather than use a form. Prescriptions Separate new prescriptions, repeat prescriptions, and online options clearly. Patient Access Explain online booking like a service, not an administrative maze. Contact the practice Route admin queries safely and explain what the form should and should not be used for.

What changes in this refreshed approach

The site becomes less of a brochure and more of a practical, patient-task-led service surface.

A

Appointments become the lead action

The homepage no longer gives every route equal visual weight. The most common patient need leads the experience.

U

Urgent guidance becomes more explicit

Patients are told early what counts as urgent, what to do on the day, and when to phone immediately.

C

Contact routes are easier to trust

Visible labels, clearer purpose, and response expectations make the contact experience feel safer and more modern.

P

Practice information is still easy to find

Services, surgeries, team details, and registration remain prominent — just better organised around user intent.

Patients

Clearer patient tasks

Appointments, prescriptions, registration, and Patient Access are grouped as practical tasks, not scattered as equal-weight homepage boxes.
