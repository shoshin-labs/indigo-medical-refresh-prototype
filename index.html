Homepage priorities in this refresh

The point is not to add more content. It is to organise existing services around patient intent.

1. Make appointments obvious

Promote appointment booking above everything else, with direct routes for routine, urgent, and out-of-hours support.

2. Keep the reassuring tone

Maintain the practice’s warm, clinically trustworthy feel, but remove the need to scan long paragraphs for practical instructions.

3. Reduce avoidable contact friction

Show what the contact form is for, what it is not for, and when the patient should phone instead.

Contact concept

Make the right contact route obvious

Rather than saying patients can “complete the form or email” without showing a clear route, this concept separates contact types and adds expectations.

  • Routine appointment queries
    Use the appointments route or phone reception.
  • Urgent medical problems
    Call the practice directly. Do not use the form.
  • Admin questions
    Use the contact form for non-urgent administrative queries.
