Prescriptions page concept

Make prescription requests clearer, calmer, and more task-specific.

The public Indigo Medical site already separates prescription information, but this refreshed concept would present the pathways more cleanly: new prescriptions, repeat prescriptions, digital ordering, and patient messaging.

New prescriptions

Explain the route for first-time requests

Patients should understand quickly whether a prescription needs a consultation, review, or follow-up from the practice team.

Repeat prescriptions

Make repeat ordering the simplest pathway

For repeat items, the page can encourage the fastest route available, including Patient Access where appropriate.

Patient Access ordering

Explain clearly how online prescription ordering works and when it is the fastest option.

Messaging and updates

Where AccuRx or SMS messaging is used, explain what messages patients may receive and what actions they may be asked to take.

Safety and expectations

Use short guidance on timing, collection, review intervals, and when a medication query needs clinician support instead of a form submission.
