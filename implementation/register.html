Register with us

Register with Indigo Medical.

Before you begin, check what information you need, how to send your details and what will happen next.

1

Check the registration requirements

Make sure you have the documents and details needed before you start your registration.

2

Send your form or speak to reception

Use the registration route provided by the practice and contact reception if you are unsure which option to use.

3

Wait for confirmation

The practice will review your registration and let you know when you can begin using services.

Helpful to know

If you are new to the area, changing practice or helping a relative register, the practice team can guide you through the process.

Need help before you start?

If you have a question about eligibility, documents or how to register, contact the practice before you submit your details.
