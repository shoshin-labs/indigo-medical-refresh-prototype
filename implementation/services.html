Services

Healthcare services for every stage of family life.

Indigo Medical offers everyday general practice, nurse-led clinics and specialist services across Jersey. Browse the services below and contact the practice if you are unsure which route is right for you.

1

General practice

See a GP for routine concerns, ongoing conditions, advice, referrals and day-to-day family medicine.

2

Nurse-led care

Practice nurses and other clinicians support screenings, immunisations, checks, travel care and follow-up treatment.

3

Specialist support

Indigo Medical also offers services such as dermatology, ear microsuction, pharmacist support and corporate medicals.

Browse our services

If you are not sure where to start, contact reception and the team can guide you.

Antenatal services

Support before and during pregnancy, alongside your wider maternity care.

Cervical screening

Routine screening appointments in a calm, professional setting.

Child immunisation clinics

Routine childhood vaccinations and preventive care for growing families.

Clinical pharmacist service

Medication reviews, prescription support and safer long-term medicines management.

Corporate medicals

Occupational and work-related medical support for employers and employees.

Dermatology

Assessment and treatment support for a range of skin concerns.

Ear microsuction

Ear wax removal and hearing-related support delivered in practice.

Practice nurse clinics

Blood pressure checks, blood tests, wound care, monitoring and other nurse-led appointments.

Travel clinics

Travel health advice, vaccinations and planning before you go away.

Need help choosing a service?

If you are unsure whether you need a GP, nurse clinic or a more specialist appointment, contact the practice and explain what you need help with.
