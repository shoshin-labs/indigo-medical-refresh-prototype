Patient Access

Online access explained simply.

Patients should be able to understand what Patient Access is for, how to get started, and what to do if they need help.

A

What can I use it for?

Use Patient Access for supported routine tasks such as online appointments and repeat prescriptions where available.

B

Who is it for?

Existing patients who want a digital route for straightforward practice tasks without calling every time.

C

What should be clearer?

What identification is needed, how registration details are issued, and which appointment types can actually be booked online.

1

Request access

Tell patients whether they need to contact reception, complete a form or collect details from the practice, and what ID to have ready.

2

Receive your registration details

Set clear expectations about what the practice team will send and how long setup is likely to take.

3

Sign in and start using the service

Show what patients can do after activation and where to get help if something does not work first time.
