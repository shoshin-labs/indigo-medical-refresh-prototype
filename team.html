Team page refresh

Turn the team page into reassurance, not just a directory.

The public Indigo Medical site already separates doctors and clinical staff categories. This concept keeps that structure, but presents it with more warmth, clarity, and patient confidence.

Senior Partners

Present experienced leadership clearly, with a calm summary of special interests, values, or areas of continuity if desired.

Partners

Help patients understand the breadth of ongoing GP support and choice of doctor without overwhelming the page with dense profile text.

General Practitioner

Use short, patient-friendly profiles that feel human and approachable while staying clinically credible.

Trainee General Practitioner

Explain training status clearly and confidently so patients understand what to expect and feel reassured.

Clinical staff

Show nurse and wider clinical support roles in a way that clarifies who can help with what.

Reception and support

Acknowledge front-of-house and support staff as part of the patient experience, especially given how central reception is to appointments and triage.

Presentation note

For a practice website, the team page is part trust signal, part orientation tool.

Patients are often checking who they may see, whether they can choose a preferred doctor, and what kind of care culture the practice has. A more polished team page can do a lot of reassurance work without becoming over-designed.
