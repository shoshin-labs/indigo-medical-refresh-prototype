Why it works better

Patients can compare locations more quickly.

Instead of scanning large text blocks, patients can see the main facts for each surgery in parallel: where it is, when it opens, and how to contact the practice.

Opportunity

Use location pages to reduce avoidable calls.

Adding cleaner maps links, parking notes, wheelchair access details, and what services are routinely offered at each location could save confusion and improve first impressions.
