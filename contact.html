Contact us

Choose the right contact route.

Separating urgent care from administrative enquiries helps patients get where they need to go faster and reduces the risk of using the wrong route.

Before you contact us

Use the form for admin. Use the phone for urgent care.

  • Routine appointment queries
    Use the appointments route or call reception on 01534 730541.
  • Urgent same-day problems
    Call the practice directly and do not use the form.
  • General admin questions
    Use the form or email for non-urgent queries such as registration or Patient Access help.
  • Out-of-hours support
    Call 01534 445445 when the practice is closed and urgent help is needed.
