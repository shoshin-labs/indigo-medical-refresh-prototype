Contact page refresh

Make the right contact route obvious before the patient writes anything.

This concept separates urgent medical needs from administrative enquiries, adds visible form labels, and makes the practice’s direct contact details easier to trust and use.

Contact guidance

Use the form for admin. Use the phone for urgent care.

  • Routine appointment queries
    Use the appointments route or call reception on 01534 730541.
  • Urgent same-day problems
    Call the practice directly and do not use the form.
  • General admin queries
    Use the form or email for non-urgent questions.
  • Out of hours support
    Call 01534 445445 when the practice is closed and urgent help is needed.
