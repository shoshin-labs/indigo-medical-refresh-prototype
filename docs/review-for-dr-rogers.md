# Indigo Medical website review

## Short review
Indigo Medical’s current website feels professional, trustworthy, and clinically reassuring. It gives patients the right broad information, but it is stronger as an information site than as a task-completion site.

The main opportunity is to make practical journeys — especially booking appointments — clearer, quicker, and more confidence-inspiring. At present, patients often need to read through paragraphs to work out the correct next step for routine care, urgent same-day issues, online booking, and out-of-hours support.

There is also an opportunity to modernise the online-booking journey. The current Patient Access registration path appears administrative and dated, with multiple steps and a downloadable Word document. Even if the underlying process must stay the same operationally, it could be presented much more simply.

Finally, a few usability and accessibility refinements would improve confidence further: clearer contact options, visible form labels, more explicit calls to action, and small copy edits for polish.

## Summary
The practice already has a credible digital presence. The next step is not a complete reinvention, but a refresh that helps patients complete their most important tasks with less effort and more certainty.
